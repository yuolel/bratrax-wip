03 — THE LOGO

The Inline Mark

Single-line wordmark: BRATRAX. in Space Mono Bold with italic T (extended diagonal stem), italic R, and acid green dot. Naming convention: "Light" and "Dark" refer to the logo color, not the background — Light logo (light text) goes on dark backgrounds; Dark logo (dark text) goes on light backgrounds.

On near black (primary)
On off black
On white (inverted)
The inline mark is the primary and only logo. Use Light Inline on dark backgrounds, Dark Inline on light backgrounds. The acid green dot is never optional — always #D4FF00. The italic T stem and italic R are defining brand elements — never straighten them. Minimum size: 14px cap height. Clear space: at least 1× the cap height on all sides.
04 — SERIF ACCENT

Single-Word Emphasis

One word per headline is set in DM Serif Display italic. It inherits the parent size. The word should carry the insight, not be filler. Always acid green.

Not another dashboard tool

Accent word: "another" — carries the dismissal

What everyone skips

Accent word: "skips" — carries the insight
Wrong: "The analytics protocol system" — too many words. One word only.

11 — TEXTURE SYSTEM

Visual Textures

Three texture layers create the editorial, screen-print energy. Graph paper is always-on. Halftone and red dither are decorative.

GRAPH PAPER GRID
HALFTONE DITHER
RED DITHER SQUARE
Graph paper: acid green lines at 4% opacity, 40px grid, fixed overlay.
Halftone: radial-gradient dots 1.5px at 15% opacity, 8px spacing.
Red square: rotated 12deg, red dots 2px at 25% opacity, 6px spacing. Used sparingly for tension.
