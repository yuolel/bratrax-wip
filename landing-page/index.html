01 — The Problem

Same word.
Three meanings.
Zero alignment.

Marketing
"Conversion"
Someone submitted a form. Lead captured. Pipeline moving.
Finance
"Conversion"
Payment cleared. Revenue recognized. Money in the account.
CEO
"Conversion"
A new paying customer. Growth. Proof the business works.

Everyone's right. Everyone's using different definitions. Nobody noticed until the numbers stopped agreeing — and by then, you've spent six months making decisions based on a word that meant three different things to the people who voted on it.

02 — The Ontology

Before we build a single dashboard, we define the ontology — your team's written agreement about what things mean, how they relate, and what's worth tracking. If something isn't in the ontology, it doesn't exist in the system. That's not a limitation. That's the point.
