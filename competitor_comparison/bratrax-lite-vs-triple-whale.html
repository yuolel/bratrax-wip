02 — Head-to-head

Comparison guide: Bratrax Lite vs Triple Whale minus the fluff.

These dimensions matter once you admit whether you truly need Whale’s sprawling OS—or just decisive Shopify attribution.

Pricing: Flat vs. scales with your revenue

Bratrax Lite

$99/mo flat · lite.bratrax.com · April 2026 — Shopify GMV shocks never touch Bratrax’s subscription line item.

Triple Whale

Hybrid GMV-plus-plan math produces ~$1,129/mo · triplewhale.com/pricing on the cited $5M GMV tier (April 2026) atop free entry SKUs suited for exploratory brands.

Why it matters: At $5M GMV, the gap is $99/mo vs $1,129/mo (as of April 2026 · lite.bratrax.com, triplewhale.com/pricing) — an ~11x difference. Triple Whale’s pricing model is built on the same logic as most enterprise SaaS: as you grow, you pay more. Bratrax’s flat model reflects a different product structure — no sales team, no CS org scaled to your GMV, no reason to bill like one.

Scope: Attribution layer vs. full intelligence platform

Bratrax Lite

Laser Shopify D2C attribution with five models baked in plus dashboards tuned for roughly $500K–$50M brands — TikTok/Klaviyo next.

Triple Whale

Stacks Moby AI, creatives, forecasting, activation, warehouses, consultancy — Whale markets itself as the “everything” OS for ecommerce.

Why it matters: A broader platform is only valuable if you use the breadth. For a $3M Shopify brand with one or two people running ads, most of that surface goes unused — and the pricing reflects the full platform whether you use it or not. For a $15M brand with a marketing team that needs creative analytics, forecasting, and activation in the same workflow, Triple Whale’s consolidation argument is real.

AI: Your model vs. their Moby

Bratrax Lite

You wire Claude or ChatGPT with your API key — interrogations stay portable and vendor-neutral atop Bratrax’s dataset.

Triple Whale

Moby Chat + Agents run inside Whale’s walled garden—“not bolted ChatGPT” per their storyline—pushing judgments through their modeled stack.

Why it matters: Triple Whale is not just giving you AI access to your data — it is giving you Triple Whale’s AI interpreting Triple Whale’s data model inside Triple Whale’s system. That can be useful if you want a built-in ecommerce agent, but it also means the assumptions, context, and recommendations stay inside their platform. Bratrax takes the opposite approach: we provide the attribution data layer, and you bring the AI you already trust to question it on your own terms.

Onboarding: Focused tool vs. broader platform setup

Bratrax Lite

Zero pixel drama: connect Shopify, Meta, Google, and Bratrax opens dashboards instantly—narrow scope kills busywork.

Triple Whale

Self-service friendly with Triple Pixel installs and “~15 minutes” lore, then unlocks onboarding specialists plus CSMs as plans scale breadth.

Why it matters: The relevant contrast here is not really fast vs. slow — it is narrow tool vs. broad platform. A focused attribution product has fewer pieces to set up by design. Triple Whale’s setup is lighter than most enterprise platforms, but it is still configuring a multi-product suite.

Data portability

Bratrax Lite

Exports are first-class—you own the stitched dataset versus renting another UI shell.

Triple Whale

Managed warehousing exists for elites, yet garden-variety plans bury export certainty—assume you clarify before contracts.

Why it matters: The dashboard is not the real product — the dataset behind it is. Every report, filter, and breakdown you use is constrained by how the vendor modeled your data before you opened the tool. If your attribution history, customer logic, and reporting workflows live inside a vendor’s platform, switching later means rebuilding more than dashboards; it means rebuilding the data layer those dashboards depend on. Bratrax takes the opposite position: the dataset should be portable and usable outside the UI, so your analytics does not depend on renting one vendor’s view of your business.

Service & support: self-serve vs. analyst-supported

Bratrax Lite

Operators run workflows solo—documentation-focused setup, no analyst or CSM layer.

Triple Whale

Implementation partners and dedicated CSMs unlock as tiers climb—modeled for hands-on fleets (pricing page · April 2026).

Why it matters: If your team wants a partner-supported workflow with structured account guidance, Triple Whale has that path. If you want to stay self-serve and avoid service layers in the operating model, Bratrax Lite is the cleaner fit.

05 — When Triple Whale is the right choice

There are cases where Triple Whale is the better fit.

  • You want a feature-complete ecommerce intelligence platform. Triple Whale publicly positions as replacing 10+ tools — attribution, creative analytics, forecasting, activation, MMM, and more under one roof. If you want breadth and consolidation, Bratrax Lite is deliberately narrower.
  • You need MMM, incrementality testing, or forecasting. Bratrax Lite does not have these. Triple Whale’s platform includes them. For brands at a scale where those measurement methods add meaningful signal, Triple Whale’s investment is easier to justify.
  • You want a built-in AI agent ecosystem, not BYO AI. Moby Chat and Moby Agents are purpose-built for ecommerce workflows. If you want AI that is pre-trained for your use case rather than a general-purpose model you bring yourself, Triple Whale’s approach makes more sense.
  • You are spending $200K+/month on ads and need the full platform depth. The GMV-scaled pricing is harder to justify at $1M–$5M revenue. At $10M–$30M GMV, the analytics complexity, team size, and measurement needs are more likely to match what Triple Whale has built.
  • You want a dedicated Customer Success Manager and implementation support. Higher Triple Whale tiers include structured onboarding and account management. Bratrax Lite is self-serve — there is no CSM included.
06 — Migration notes

Switching tools means a parallel-run period.

Bratrax Lite is self-serve — no migration call, no implementation specialist. Connect Shopify, Meta, and Google Ads at launch. Historical data from Triple Whale is not auto-imported; attribution history in Bratrax Lite starts from the day you connect.

If you have built reporting, custom dashboards, or workflows on top of Triple Whale’s data — particularly if you are on a managed warehouse tier — factor in what stays with the platform and what you take with you before cutting over.

07 — FAQ

Frequently asked alternative questions on Triple Whale versus Bratrax Lite.

How is Bratrax Lite different from Triple Whale?

Bratrax Lite is a self-serve, flat-priced Shopify D2C attribution product. Triple Whale is a full ecommerce intelligence platform covering attribution, AI agents, creative analytics, forecasting, activation, and more — with GMV-based pricing. Different scope, different pricing model, different buyer.

Is Bratrax Lite cheaper because it does less?

Yes, honestly. Bratrax Lite does not have Moby Agents, MMM, incrementality testing, forecasting, creative analytics, or Sonar activation. It does five attribution models well for Shopify D2C brands running Facebook and Google. Triple Whale’s feature surface is genuinely broader. The question is whether you need that breadth — and whether the pricing gap at your GMV is justified by how much of it you use.

Does Bratrax Lite have a free plan like Triple Whale?

No free plan. Bratrax Lite starts at $99/mo flat · lite.bratrax.com (as of April 2026). Triple Whale’s free plan · triplewhale.com/pricing exists but is limited — for a brand running paid ads across multiple channels, the paid tier is where attribution features live · April 2026.

Does Bratrax Lite have something like Triple Whale’s Moby?

Not a proprietary AI agent. Bratrax Lite lets you connect your own Claude or ChatGPT with your own API key. You bring the AI; Bratrax provides the data. Triple Whale’s Moby is a deeper, ecommerce-specific AI product — more integrated, more opinionated, and tied to the Triple Whale platform.

What attribution models does Bratrax Lite include?

All five: first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay, and position-based. All included, no tier unlock required. Triple Whale has 7+ models including AI-powered options like Total Impact.

Can I try Bratrax Lite before committing?

Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee, no annual contract, cancel anytime.
