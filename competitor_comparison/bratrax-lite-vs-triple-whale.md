# Bratrax Lite vs Triple Whale: The Honest Comparison

*Last updated: May 1, 2026*

**Bratrax Lite is a flat-priced ($99/mo) attribution tool built for Shopify D2C brands that want portable data and the ability to bring their own Claude or ChatGPT.**

**Triple Whale is a GMV-priced ecommerce intelligence platform combining attribution, AI agents (Moby), creative analytics, MMM, and forecasting under one roof.**

Built by the team that's run paid attribution for D2C brands at Inceptly for eight years.

**Subhead:** Flat $99/mo vs $1,129/mo at $5M GMV (as of April 2026). A Shopify attribution tool vs a platform built to replace 10 others. What that gap means for your decision.

## TL;DR

- **Bratrax Lite** is for Shopify D2C brands ($500K–$50M) that want flat $99/mo pricing, self-serve setup, portable data, and the ability to use their own Claude or ChatGPT with their own API key.
- **Triple Whale** is a full ecommerce intelligence platform — "Measurement. Analytics. AI. Creative. Automation. Everything you need to scale, in one easy-to-use platform" ([triplewhale.com](https://www.triplewhale.com), April 2026). Built for brands that want a broad feature set under one roof and are comfortable with GMV-scaled pricing.

- **Key decision question:** Do you need a focused attribution layer for a Shopify store — or a multi-product ecommerce intelligence platform with AI agents, activation tools, and optional consulting support?

## Head-to-Head: Bratrax Lite vs Triple Whale on the Dimensions That Actually Matter

---

### 1. Pricing: Flat vs. Scales With Your Revenue

**Bratrax Lite** charges $99/mo flat. No GMV multiplier. No annual contract required. A brand doing $1M and a brand doing $20M in Shopify revenue pay the same number.

**Triple Whale** prices by GMV and package. Their pricing page ([triplewhale.com/pricing](https://www.triplewhale.com/pricing), April 2026) shows $1,129/mo at the $5M GMV tier on the recommended paid plan. Free plan at $0/mo is available; month-to-month pricing alongside the annual default. For a brand running paid ads, the paid tier is where attribution features live.

**Why it matters:** At $5M GMV, the gap is $99/mo vs $1,129/mo — more than 11x. Triple Whale's pricing model is built on the same logic as most enterprise SaaS: as you grow, you pay more. Bratrax's flat model reflects a different product structure — no sales team, no CS org scaled to your GMV, no reason to bill like one. Whether the feature breadth Triple Whale offers is worth it depends on how much of the platform you'll actually use.

---

### 2. Scope: Attribution Layer vs. Full Intelligence Platform

**Bratrax Lite** is purpose-built for Shopify D2C attribution. Five attribution models, pre-built dashboards for the $1M–$20M brand, connectors for Shopify, Meta, and Google Ads. No creative tools, no forecasting, no activation layer. Opinionated and focused.

**Triple Whale** positions as "the complete intelligence platform for ecommerce" — explicitly replacing 10+ tools ([triplewhale.com](https://www.triplewhale.com), April 2026). Beyond attribution: Moby Chat, Moby Agents, MMM, incrementality testing, forecasting, Sonar, creative analytics, and optional managed data warehouse.

**Why it matters:** A broader platform is only valuable if you use the breadth. For a $3M Shopify brand with one or two people running ads, most of that surface goes unused — and the pricing reflects the full platform whether you use it or not. For a $15M brand with a marketing team that needs creative analytics, forecasting, and activation in the same workflow, Triple Whale's consolidation argument is real.

---

### 3. AI: Your Model vs. Their Moby

**Bratrax Lite** lets you connect your own Claude or ChatGPT with your own API key. No proprietary chatbot, no token markup, no AI layer tied to the vendor. Bratrax provides the data layer. You bring the intelligence layer.

**Triple Whale** built Moby Chat and Moby Agents as proprietary AI products. Their positioning: "AI that actually works for ecommerce — this isn't ChatGPT with your data bolted on" ([triplewhale.com](https://www.triplewhale.com), April 2026). Moby Agents automate complex analyses within their platform.

**Why it matters:** Triple Whale is specifically arguing against the "ChatGPT with your data bolted on" approach — and Bratrax's bring-your-own-AI setup is, from the outside, exactly that. If you want an AI agent already trained for ecommerce workflows and integrated into the same platform as your data, Moby is more complete. If you already pay for Claude or ChatGPT and want to use it against your attribution data without an additional layer to trust, Bratrax keeps it simpler. The tradeoff is genuine.

---

### 4. Onboarding: Focused Tool vs. Broader Platform Setup

**Bratrax Lite** is self-serve end-to-end. No pixel install, no demo call. Connect Shopify, Meta, and Google Ads — live dashboard. Narrow scope means setup is correspondingly simple.

**Triple Whale** doesn't require a demo call — you can start on the free plan without talking to anyone. Their site says "Most brands are up and running with Triple Whale in 15 minutes, with no engineer required" ([triplewhale.com](https://www.triplewhale.com), April 2026). Setup includes installing the Triple Pixel; higher paid tiers unlock Implementation Specialist onboarding and a Dedicated CSM ([triplewhale.com/pricing](https://www.triplewhale.com/pricing), April 2026).

**Why it matters:** The contrast is not really fast vs. slow — it is narrow tool vs. broad platform. A focused attribution product has fewer pieces to set up by design. Triple Whale's setup is lighter than most enterprise platforms, but it is still configuring a multi-product suite. For a brand that wants to be running attribution in an afternoon, that distinction matters.

---

### 5. Data Portability

**Bratrax Lite** is built around portable, exportable data. The dataset is the deliverable, not just the dashboard.

**Triple Whale** offers a managed data warehouse at higher tiers ([triplewhale.com/pricing](https://www.triplewhale.com/pricing), April 2026). What that includes in terms of export and portability for standard plans is not publicly specified. If portability matters, ask Triple Whale directly before committing.

**Why it matters:** There is a difference between a product that offers some data access and one designed around portability as a core promise. Bratrax's position is the latter. Your attribution data, exportable and usable outside the dashboard — that is the product.

---

### 6. Service & Support: Self-Serve vs. CSM-Supported

**Bratrax Lite** is self-serve — no CSM, no dedicated support tier, no implementation specialist. Documentation and self-serve onboarding. This is a real tradeoff, not a hidden weakness.

**Triple Whale** includes an Implementation Specialist at onboarding and a Dedicated Customer Success Manager at higher tiers ([triplewhale.com/pricing](https://www.triplewhale.com/pricing), April 2026). That CSM provides ongoing account management, consultation, and access to structured support as the platform grows in complexity.

**Why it matters:** Dedicated CSM support is a real differentiator for teams that want expert guidance embedded in the tool relationship. For brands at $10M–$30M GMV with a full marketing team activating Triple Whale's breadth, that structured support pays for itself. Bratrax Lite trades it for self-serve speed and a flat price — but the tradeoff is real.

## Who Bratrax Lite Is For

- **Shopify D2C brands at $500K–$50M revenue.** Strongest fit at $1M–$20M. If your primary channel is Shopify and you're running paid ads on Facebook and Google, Bratrax Lite is built for your stack.
- **Founders and media buyers who want self-serve.** No demo call, no implementation specialist. If you want attribution running in an afternoon, the self-serve model is the point.
- **Teams that want portable data and BYO-AI.** If you already pay for Claude or ChatGPT and want to query your attribution data with your own model — rather than a vendor's proprietary AI — Bratrax Lite is built for that workflow.
- **Brands that want flat, predictable pricing.** $99/mo regardless of GMV or revenue growth. At $5M GMV, that's more than $1,000/mo less than Triple Whale's standard paid tier.

## When Triple Whale Is the Right Choice

- **You want a feature-complete ecommerce intelligence platform.** Triple Whale publicly positions as replacing 10+ tools — attribution, creative analytics, forecasting, activation, MMM, and more under one roof ([triplewhale.com](https://www.triplewhale.com), April 2026). If you want breadth and consolidation, Bratrax Lite is deliberately narrower.
- **You need MMM, incrementality testing, or forecasting.** Bratrax Lite does not have these. Triple Whale's platform includes them. For brands at a scale where those measurement methods add meaningful signal, Triple Whale's investment is easier to justify.
- **You want a built-in AI agent ecosystem, not BYO AI.** Moby Chat and Moby Agents are purpose-built for ecommerce workflows. If you want AI pre-trained for your use case rather than a general-purpose model you bring yourself, Triple Whale's approach makes more sense.
- **You are spending $200K+/month on ads and need the full platform depth.** The GMV-scaled pricing is harder to justify at $1M–$5M revenue. At $10M–$30M GMV, the analytics complexity, team size, and measurement needs are more likely to match what Triple Whale has built.
- **You want a dedicated Customer Success Manager and implementation support.** Higher Triple Whale tiers include structured onboarding and account management. Bratrax Lite is self-serve — there is no CSM included.

## Migration Notes

Switching attribution tools means running two setups in parallel through a transition window.

Bratrax Lite is self-serve — no migration call, no implementation specialist. Connects to Shopify, Meta, and Google Ads at launch. Historical data from Triple Whale is not auto-imported; attribution history in Bratrax Lite starts from the day you connect.

If you have built reporting, custom dashboards, or workflows on top of Triple Whale's data — particularly if you are on a managed warehouse tier — factor in what stays with the platform and what you take with you before cutting over.

## FAQ

**How is Bratrax Lite different from Triple Whale?**
Bratrax Lite is a self-serve, flat-priced Shopify D2C attribution product. Triple Whale is a full ecommerce intelligence platform covering attribution, AI agents, creative analytics, forecasting, activation, and more — with GMV-based pricing. Different scope, different pricing model, different buyer.

**Is Bratrax Lite cheaper because it does less?**
Yes, honestly. Bratrax Lite does not have Moby Agents, MMM, incrementality testing, forecasting, creative analytics, or Sonar activation. It does five attribution models well for Shopify D2C brands running Facebook and Google. Triple Whale's feature surface is genuinely broader. The question is whether you need that breadth — and whether the pricing gap at your GMV is justified by how much of it you use.

**Does Bratrax Lite have a free plan like Triple Whale?**
No free plan. Bratrax Lite starts at $99/mo. Triple Whale's free plan exists but is limited — for a brand running paid ads across multiple channels, the paid tier is where attribution features live.

**Does Bratrax Lite have something like Triple Whale's Moby?**
Not a proprietary AI agent. Bratrax Lite lets you connect your own Claude or ChatGPT with your own API key. You bring the AI; Bratrax provides the data. Triple Whale's Moby is a deeper, ecommerce-specific AI product — more integrated, more opinionated, and tied to the Triple Whale platform.

**What attribution models does Bratrax Lite include?**
All five: first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay, and position-based. All included, no tier unlock required. Triple Whale has 7+ models including AI-powered options like Total Impact.

**Can I try Bratrax Lite before committing?**
Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee, no annual contract, cancel anytime.
